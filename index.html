About the Project

Pixel Paw is an innovative AR communication tool designed to create meaningful connections in urban environments. Born from cutting-edge research in heterotopia and urban serendipity, our platform reimagines how people interact with space and each other.

Research Foundation

Grounded in "Heterotopia" research on urban serendipity: "Place-Making & Navigating The Otherness in Tokyo: A Case Study of Locative Media Mobile Application" (Yeung, H.T. Leonardo Abstracts Service, ID: 4136)

Why Pixel Paw Exists

Today's society faces a silent crisis of social disengagement. Amid social media overload, many people feel exhausted by superficial connections while losing opportunities for meaningful co-creation.

The Three Core Problems We Solve:

1. The Lost Talent of the Socially Reluctant
  • Traditional networking favors those good at self-promotion and small talk
  • People with deep expertise and passion but social anxiety remain undervalued
2. The Untapped Potential of "Oshi Culture"
  • Japan's fan culture represents a powerful engine for trust and collaboration
  • Yet it remains confined to personal consumption, never transforming into social creativity
3. The Missing Infrastructure for Meaningful Achievements
  • No system exists that values "what we've accomplished together" over credentials
  • People lack ways to prove their identity as co-creators beyond corporate roles

Our Solution

Pixel Paw transforms urban spaces into "Oshi Ecosystems" using AR and Soulbound Tokens (SBT). Starting from shared passions, we've built a protocol where anyone can naturally unlock their social potential through concrete collaborative projects.
